<p><p>The liquid butter alternatives market comprises primarily two types: palm oil type and lauric oil type. Palm oil-based alternatives are derived from palm fruit and are known for their stability and versatility in cooking, often used for frying and baking. Lauric oil type, sourced from coconuts and palm kernel oil, offers a distinct fatty acid profile, providing a creamy texture and potential health benefits. Both types cater to diverse consumer preferences for healthier, plant-based options in culinary applications.</p></p>
