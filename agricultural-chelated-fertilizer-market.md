<p><p>The Agricultural Chelated Fertilizer Market comprises various types including EDTA, EDDHA, DTPA, IDHA, and others. EDTA (ethylenediaminetetraacetic acid) is widely used for micronutrient delivery, whereas EDDHA (ethylenediamine-N,N'-diacetic acid) is effective in alkaline soils. DTPA (diethylenetriaminepentaacetic acid) enhances metal ion availability, and IDHA (isobutylidene diurea) offers slow-release attributes. Other chelates may include various amino acid or organic-based formulations that improve nutrient uptake efficiency. The choice of chelate depends on soil conditions and crop requirements.</p></p>
