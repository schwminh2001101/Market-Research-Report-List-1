<p><p>The servo system controller market can be categorized mainly into single-axis and multi-axis controllers. Single-axis controllers manage one motor or actuator, ideal for applications requiring precise motion control in one dimension, such as in robotic arms or conveyor systems. In contrast, multi-axis controllers coordinate multiple motors or actuators, enabling complex motion sequences and synchronization in machinery, robotics, and automation processes. Each type serves distinct needs based on the complexity and scale of motion required in various industries.</p></p>
