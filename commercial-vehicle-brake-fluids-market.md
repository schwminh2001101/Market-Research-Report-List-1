<p><ul><li>DOT 3</li><li>DOT 4</li><li>DOT 5</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The commercial vehicle brake fluids market features several types, primarily categorized as DOT 3, DOT 4, and DOT 5. DOT 3 is a glycol-based fluid, offering adequate performance for standard braking systems but has a lower boiling point. DOT 4 enhances heat resistance, making it suitable for higher-performance vehicles. DOT 5 utilizes a silicone base, known for its non-hygroscopic properties, ideal for specialized applications. Each type meets specific performance standards and is designed to ensure safe and effective braking in various conditions.</p></p>
