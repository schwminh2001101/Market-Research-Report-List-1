<p><p>A reciprocating piston compressor is a type of positive displacement compressor that utilizes a piston driven by a crankshaft to compress gases. This compressor operates by drawing gas into a cylinder, compressing it as the piston moves downward, and discharging it at high pressure when the piston moves upward. It is widely used in various applications, including refrigeration, air conditioning, and industrial processes.</p><p>The reciprocating piston compressor market is experiencing significant growth, driven by rising demand across several end-user industries, including automotive, oil and gas, and manufacturing.
