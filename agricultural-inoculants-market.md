<p><p>The agricultural inoculants market is divided into two main types: seed inoculants and soil inoculants. Seed inoculants are applied directly to seeds before planting to enhance germination and promote beneficial microbial activity, boosting plant growth and resistance to diseases. Soil inoculants, on the other hand, are introduced into the soil to improve nutrient availability, enhance soil health, and promote beneficial microbial communities. Both types play a crucial role in sustainable agriculture by optimizing crop yields and soil fertility.</p></p>
