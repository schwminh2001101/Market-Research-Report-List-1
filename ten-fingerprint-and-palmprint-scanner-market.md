<p><p>The ten-fingerprint and palmprint scanner market is categorized into optical scanning and vein scanning. Optical scanning uses light to capture fingerprint or palm patterns, providing high-resolution images for identification. This method is widely used due to its cost-effectiveness and reliability. In contrast, vein scanning utilizes infrared light to detect unique vascular patterns beneath the skin, offering enhanced security and resistance to spoofing. Both technologies are crucial in biometrics for secure access and identification in various sectors.</p></p>
