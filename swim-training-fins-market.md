<p><p>The swim training fins market is primarily divided into three types: short blade swim fins, long blade swim fins, and monofins. Short blade swim fins enhance kick efficiency and are ideal for sprint training and technique improvement. Long blade swim fins promote strength and endurance by providing increased resistance, making them suitable for distance training. Monofins, typically used in synchronized swimming and freediving, allow for a more fluid movement and improve propulsion through a unified kick, enhancing overall swimming performance.</p></p>
