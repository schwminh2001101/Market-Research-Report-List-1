<p><p>The V Port Ball Valves market is categorized into two main types: Soft Seal Valves and Hard Seal Valves. Soft Seal Valves utilize an elastomeric seal, providing excellent tightness and allowing for significant flow regulation at lower pressures; they are ideal for applications requiring dynamic sealing. Hard Seal Valves, on the other hand, feature a metal-to-metal sealing mechanism, offering enhanced durability and resistance to high temperatures and pressures, making them suitable for harsher environments and critical operations.</p></p>
