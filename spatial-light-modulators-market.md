<p><p>The Spatial Light Modulators (SLM) market is divided into two main types: reflective SLM and transmissive SLM. Reflective SLMs manipulate light reflected off their surfaces, enhancing contrast and brightness, making them ideal for high-resolution displays and projectors. In contrast, transmissive SLMs control light passing through them, offering advantages in applications like liquid crystal displays and optical filtering. Each type serves distinct applications across industries, including consumer electronics, telecommunications, and biomedical imaging, contributing to diverse technological advancements.</p></p>
