<p><p>The thermoelectric cooler and assemblies market is primarily categorized into single-stage and multistage modules. Single-stage modules are designed for simple cooling applications, utilizing a single thermoelectric element to transfer heat efficiently. They are commonly used in portable coolers and small electronics. In contrast, multistage modules consist of multiple single-stage units stacked together, enabling them to achieve lower temperatures. These are ideal for applications requiring significant cooling, such as laser systems and refrigeration in scientific equipment.</p></p>
