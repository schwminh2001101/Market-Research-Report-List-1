<p><p>The hair dye products market is categorized into three primary types: temporary, semi-permanent, and permanent hair dyes. Temporary hair dyes provide short-lived color that typically lasts until the next wash, ideal for experimentation. Semi-permanent dyes offer color lasting several weeks and gradually fade, suitable for users seeking longer-lasting effects without the commitment of permanence. Permanent hair dyes penetrate the hair shaft, delivering enduring color that requires regrowth or significant fading before refreshing, making them popular for long-term use.</p></p>
