<p><p>The low-pressure sealed pressure sensors market includes various types catering to specific applications. Strain-type sensors use deformation of materials to measure pressure changes, providing high sensitivity. Piezoresistive sensors exploit the change in electrical resistance of materials under stress, making them suitable for precise measurements. Capacitive sensors measure pressure through variations in capacitance, offering high accuracy and stability. Other types may include optical and resonant sensors, expanding the market by fulfilling unique requirements in various industries such as automotive, aerospace, and industrial automation.</p></p>
