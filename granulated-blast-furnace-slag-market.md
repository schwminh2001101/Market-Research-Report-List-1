Alkalinity slag, typically produced in basic oxygen furnaces, has a higher pH and is valued for its cementitious properties and ability to enhance concrete durability. In contrast, acidic slag, generated in more acidic processes, is less common and may be utilized in specific applications where its unique chemical composition is advantageous. Each type serves distinct market demands based on its properties.</p></p>
