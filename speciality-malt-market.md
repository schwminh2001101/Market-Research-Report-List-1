<p><p>The specialty malt market consists of various types, primarily roasted malts and caramelized malts. Caramelized malts, on the other hand, undergo a kilning process that develops sweet, sugary flavors and enhances color, commonly used in a variety of beers to add complexity. Together, they play a crucial role in defining the flavor profiles and visual characteristics of craft beverages.</p></p>
