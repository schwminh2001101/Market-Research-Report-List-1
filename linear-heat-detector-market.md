<p><p>The Linear Heat Detector (LHD) market primarily consists of two types: fiber optic and cable-based detectors. Fiber optic LHDs utilize optical fibers to sense temperature changes along their length, offering high sensitivity and versatility for large areas. Cable-based LHDs employ conductive materials within a cable to detect heat, making them effective for specific applications like tunnels and warehouses. Both types ensure early fire detection, enhancing safety in various environments, including industrial, commercial, and residential settings.</p></p>
