<p><p>The Viscose Carbon Fiber market is categorized into three main types: continuous, long, and short fibers. Long fibers, while shorter than continuous, still provide good mechanical properties and are often used in various industrial applications. Each type serves distinct applications based on performance requirements and manufacturing processes.</p></p>
