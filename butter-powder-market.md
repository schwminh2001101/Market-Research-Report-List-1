<p><p>The butter powder market is divided into two primary types: salted butter powder and unsalted butter powder. Salted butter powder contains added salt, enhancing flavor and extending shelf life, making it ideal for savory applications and snack foods. Unsalted butter powder, on the other hand, is pure butter without salt, allowing for greater control over seasoning in recipes, often preferred in baking and gourmet cooking. Both types cater to diverse culinary needs across the food industry.</p></p>
