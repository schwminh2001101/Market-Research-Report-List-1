As healthcare continues to prioritize effective wound care management, the demand for hydrophilic dressings is experiencing significant growth.</p><p>The Hydrophilic Wound Dressings Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 5.3% during the forecast period, driven by factors such as rising incidences of chronic wounds, an aging population, and increased awareness regarding advanced wound care solutions.
