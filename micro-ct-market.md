<p><p>The Micro-CT market is divided into two main types: structural imaging and functional imaging. Structural imaging focuses on acquiring detailed 3D representations of an object's internal architecture, revealing features such as bone density and morphology, which are crucial for fields like materials science and biomedical research. In contrast, functional imaging assesses dynamic processes, such as blood flow and metabolic activity, allowing for the evaluation of physiological functions. Both types are essential for advancing research and diagnostic capabilities across various applications.</p></p>
