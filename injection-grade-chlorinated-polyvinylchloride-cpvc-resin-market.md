<p><p>Injection Grade Chlorinated Polyvinylchloride (CPVC) Resin is categorized into two main types based on chlorine content: High Chlorine and Low Chlorine. High Chlorine CPVC features a greater chlorine content, enhancing its chemical resistance, thermal stability, and overall durability, making it suitable for demanding applications like pipes and fittings in industrial settings. In contrast, Low Chlorine CPVC offers a balance between flexibility and processability, ideal for applications requiring moderate chemical resistance and easier processing during injection molding.</p></p>
