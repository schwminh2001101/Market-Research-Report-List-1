<p><strong>The TiO2 Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Sulfate Process</li><li>Chloride Process</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The TiO2 market is primarily categorized into two production processes: the sulfate process and the chloride process. The sulfate process involves treating titanium ores with sulfuric acid, generating a high-quality pigment with a relatively lower cost but higher environmental impact. In contrast, the chloride process utilizes titanium feedstock and chlorine, resulting in a more refined product with superior brightness and durability, but at a higher production cost. Both methods cater to diverse applications, including coatings, plastics, and paper industries.</p></p>
