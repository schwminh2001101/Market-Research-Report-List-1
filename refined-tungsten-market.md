<p><p>The refined tungsten market encompasses three primary types: wolframite, scheelite, and ferberite. Wolframite, characterized by its iron and manganese content, is a key source for tungsten and known for its high density. Scheelite, recognized for its high tungsten content and fluorescence, is a significant ore used in producing tungsten products. Ferberite, a less common tungsten mineral, can also contribute to tungsten extraction but is typically less abundant. Together, these minerals play crucial roles in meeting industrial tungsten demands.</p></p>
