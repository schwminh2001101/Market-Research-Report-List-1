<p><p>The Zero Liquid Discharge (ZLD) solution market is categorized mainly into two types: Conventional and Hybrid. Conventional ZLD systems utilize traditional technologies like evaporation and crystallization to eliminate liquid waste, ensuring that no discharge occurs. In contrast, Hybrid ZLD systems combine various treatment methods, integrating both conventional technologies with advanced processes such as membrane filtration or advanced oxidation. This approach enhances efficiency and reduces energy consumption, making it ideal for industries seeking sustainable water management solutions while meeting stringent regulatory requirements.</p></p>
