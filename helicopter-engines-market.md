<p><p>The helicopter engines market comprises three main types: piston engines, gas turbine engines, and stamping engines. Piston engines are typically used in smaller helicopters, known for efficiency and simplicity. Gas turbine engines dominate larger, high-performance helicopters, offering greater power and efficiency for demanding operations. Stamping engines, while less common, are associated with unique propulsion systems that utilize a reciprocating motion. Each engine type serves specific operational needs, influencing the choice based on factors like size, cost, and mission requirements.</p></p>
