<p><ul><li>Electrolytic Copper Foil</li><li>Rolled Copper Foil</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Copper Foils for Flexible Printed Circuits (FPC) market primarily consists of two types: Electrolytic Copper Foil and Rolled Copper Foil. Electrolytic Copper Foil is produced through an electrochemical process, offering superior conductivity and uniform thickness, making it ideal for high-performance applications. In contrast, Rolled Copper Foil is created by mechanically rolling copper sheets, providing good ductility and strength. Both types are essential for enhancing the performance and reliability of flexible electronic devices, catering to diverse industry needs.</p></p>
