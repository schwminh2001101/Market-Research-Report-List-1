<p><strong>The Ventilated Seats Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Radial Fans Seats</li><li>Axial Fans Seats</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The ventilated seats market is primarily categorized into two types: radial fan seats and axial fan seats. Radial fan seats utilize a radial fan design, which effectively circulates air within the seat, providing a more uniform airflow and improved comfort. In contrast, axial fan seats use an axial fan configuration, which directs airflow along the axis of the fan, offering targeted cooling or heating. Both types enhance passenger comfort by managing seat temperature and reducing humidity, particularly in vehicles.</p></p>
