<p><p>Cloud-based manufacturing offers three primary market types: Software as a Service (SaaS), Platform as a Service (PaaS), and Infrastructure as a Service (IaaS). PaaS offers a platform for developers to build, test, and deploy applications, streamlining the development process. IaaS supplies virtualized computing resources, enabling manufacturers to manage servers, storage, and networks flexibly and on-demand, reducing infrastructure costs.</p></p>
