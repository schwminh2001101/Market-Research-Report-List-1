<p><p>The Civil Night Vision System market comprises two primary types: Low Light Night Vision Systems and Infrared Night Vision Systems. Low Light Night Vision Systems amplify ambient light to enhance visibility in dark conditions, ideal for situations with minimal illumination. In contrast, Infrared Night Vision Systems detect heat emitted by objects, allowing users to see in complete darkness. Both systems cater to various applications, including outdoor recreation, security, and surveillance, providing users with enhanced situational awareness in low-light environments.</p></p>
