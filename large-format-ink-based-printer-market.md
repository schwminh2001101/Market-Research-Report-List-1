<p><p>The large format ink-based printer market includes several types of inks, each serving different applications. Aqueous ink offers vibrant colors and is ideal for indoor use. Solvent ink provides durability and outdoor suitability while being resistant to fading. UV-cured ink uses ultraviolet light to dry instantly, making it suitable for various substrates. Latex ink combines eco-friendliness and versatility, suitable for both indoor and outdoor applications. Dye sublimation ink is primarily used for fabric printing, producing high-quality, durable images.</p></p>
