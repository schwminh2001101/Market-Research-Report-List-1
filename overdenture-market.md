<p><p>The overdenture market features several types catering to varying dental needs. Simple overdentures involve a removable prosthesis supported by existing natural teeth or implants. Root cap overdentures use the roots of remaining teeth for stability, enhancing retention and comfort. Telescopic crown overdentures consist of a primary crown fitted onto a tooth or implant, with a secondary crown securing the denture. Each type provides tailored solutions for patients seeking functional and aesthetic improvements in their oral health.</p></p>
