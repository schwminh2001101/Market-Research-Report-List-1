<p><p>The fibre coupled modulator market consists of various types, primarily including acousto-optic modulators and electro-optical modulators. Acousto-optic modulators utilize sound waves to control light propagation, enabling high-speed modulation for applications like telecommunications and imaging. Electro-optical modulators, on the other hand, use electric fields to alter the refractive index of materials, facilitating precise control over light signals for data transmission. Other types may include optical isolators and phase modulators, catering to diverse applications across communications, industrial, and research sectors.</p></p>
