<p><p>The Environmental Disinfection Robots market primarily features two types: UV-C (Ultraviolet-C) robots and HPV (Hydrogen Peroxide Vapor) robots. UV-C robots utilize ultraviolet light to eliminate pathogens on surfaces, effectively disrupting their DNA and rendering them harmless. HPV robots, on the other hand, disperse hydrogen peroxide vapor to sanitize environments thoroughly, penetrating hard-to-reach areas. Both technologies provide efficient, chemical-free disinfection solutions, playing vital roles in healthcare settings, hospitality, and public spaces to enhance hygiene and safety standards.</p></p>
