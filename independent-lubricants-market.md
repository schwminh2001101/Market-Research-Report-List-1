<p><p>The independent lubricants market encompasses three primary types: mineral lubricants, synthetic lubricants, and bio-based lubricants. Synthetic lubricants are chemically engineered for superior performance, offering better thermal stability and longer service life. Bio-based lubricants are derived from renewable sources, presenting an eco-friendlier option that minimizes environmental impact. Each type serves distinct applications across industries, catering to varying performance and sustainability needs.</p></p>
