<p><p>The PE beverage bottle caps market is primarily segmented into two types: injection caps and compression mold caps. Injection caps are produced using an injection molding process, allowing for intricate designs and precise dimensions, making them suitable for high-volume production. Compression mold caps, on the other hand, involve a molding method that compresses material to form the cap, resulting in a lighter, more cost-effective solution. Both types serve essential roles in ensuring product integrity and consumer convenience in beverage packaging.</p></p>
