<p><p>The moist wound dressings market includes various types designed to promote healing and protect wounds. Foam dressings provide cushioning and absorb exudate, while hydrocolloids form a gel-like layer that maintains moisture. Alginates, derived from seaweed, effectively absorb excess fluid and facilitate healing. Antimicrobial dressings prevent infection with embedded agents. Transparent films allow visibility of the wound while offering moisture retention. Hydrogels hydrate dry wounds and offer a soothing effect, making these products essential in advanced wound care management.</p></p>
