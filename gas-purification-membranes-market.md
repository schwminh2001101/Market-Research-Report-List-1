<p><p>The gas purification membranes market comprises two primary types: polymeric membranes and inorganic membranes. Polymeric membranes are flexible, lightweight, and often used for applications like natural gas processing and carbon dioxide removal due to their selective permeability. In contrast, inorganic membranes are crafted from materials such as ceramics and metals, offering superior thermal and chemical stability, making them ideal for high-temperature or harsh environments. Both types are critical for enhancing efficiency in gas separation and purification processes across various industries.</p></p>
