<p><ul><li>Front Silver Paste</li><li>Back Silver Paste</li><li>Aluminum Paste</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The solar cell electrode paste market is primarily categorized into three types: front silver paste, back silver paste, and aluminum paste. Front silver paste is applied on the front side of solar cells to enhance conductivity and light absorption, thereby boosting efficiency. Back silver paste is used on the rear side, providing electrical interconnection and support against environmental elements. Aluminum paste serves as a back contact, facilitating electron collection and improving overall cell performance, making all three essential for effective solar energy conversion.</p></p>
