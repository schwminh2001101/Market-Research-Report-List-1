<p><p>The smart swim goggles market is divided into two main types: in-goggle display and no-display options. In-goggle display goggles feature integrated screens that provide real-time data such as lap times, distance swum, and stroke count directly within the swimmer's line of sight. This type enhances performance feedback and enables swimmers to adjust their training instantly. In contrast, no-display goggles collect data without showing it during use, typically syncing with mobile apps post-swim for analysis and tracking progress.</p></p>
