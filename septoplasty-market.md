<p><ul><li>Closed Septoplasty</li><li>Open Septoplasty</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The septoplasty market encompasses two primary types: closed and open septoplasty. Closed septoplasty is a minimally invasive procedure where incisions are made within the nostrils, resulting in no visible scarring and a quicker recovery. In contrast, open septoplasty involves an external incision on the columella, providing better visibility and access for complex cases. Both techniques aim to correct a deviated septum, enhancing airflow through the nasal passages and improving overall respiratory function while catering to specific patient needs.</p></p>
