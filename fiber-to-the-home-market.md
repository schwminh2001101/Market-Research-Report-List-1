<p><ul><li>Single-mode</li><li>Multimode</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Fiber to the Home (FTTH) market types include single-mode and multimode fibers. Single-mode fiber features a small core that allows only one light path, making it ideal for long-distance transmission with minimal signal loss, commonly used by telecommunication companies. In contrast, multimode fiber has a larger core that enables multiple light paths, suitable for shorter distances, primarily employed in local networks and data centers. Both types cater to varying bandwidth needs and installation environments.</p></p>
