<p><ul><li>Straight Reverse</li><li>Incline to Reverse</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The double wall bed market can be categorized into three types based on their design and functionality: Straight, Reverse, and Incline to Reverse. A Straight wall bed folds down directly from a vertical position, ideal for compact spaces. The Reverse wall bed operates in the opposite direction, folding down horizontally, allowing for more versatile room layouts. Incline to Reverse beds blend features of both designs, providing a slanted folding mechanism that enhances accessibility and aesthetic appeal, making them suitable for various interior styles.</p></p>
