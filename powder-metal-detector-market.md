<p><p>The Powder Metal Detector market includes three primary types: Electromagnetic Induction, X-ray, and Microwave detection. Electromagnetic Induction detectors utilize magnetic fields to identify metallic contaminants in powdered materials. X-ray detectors examine products by passing X-rays through them, allowing for the detection of both metallic and non-metallic impurities. Microwave detectors utilize high-frequency microwave technology to identify inconsistencies in powder composition. Each type serves distinct applications in industries like food, pharmaceuticals, and manufacturing, ensuring product safety and quality control.</p></p>
