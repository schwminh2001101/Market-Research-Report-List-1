<p><ul><li>Leading Trailing Shoe Brake</li><li>Dual Two Trailing Shoe Brake</li><li>Dual Two Leading Shoe Brake</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The drum brake system market features several types, including leading-trailing shoe brakes, dual two trailing shoe brakes, and dual two leading shoe brakes. Leading-trailing shoe brakes utilize one shoe moving forward during braking, enhancing efficiency. Dual two trailing shoe brakes, common in light vehicles, employ two trailing shoes for a balanced braking force. In contrast, dual two leading shoe brakes optimize performance by having both shoes act forward, improving stopping power, particularly in heavy-duty applications. Each type caters to specific vehicle requirements and performance standards.</p></p>
