<p><ul><li>Mechanical Element Flexible Shaft Couplings</li><li>Elastomeric Element Couplings</li><li>Metallic Membrane/Disc Type Couplings</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The power transmission couplings market encompasses various types designed to connect shafts and transmit torque. Mechanical element flexible shaft couplings accommodate shaft misalignment and shock loads, enhancing durability. Elastomeric element couplings utilize rubber-like materials to absorb vibrations and ensure smooth operation. Metallic membrane or disc type couplings feature thin membranes or discs that allow for angular, parallel, and axial misalignment while providing high torque capacity. Together, these couplings play vital roles in diverse industrial applications, improving performance and reliability.</p></p>
