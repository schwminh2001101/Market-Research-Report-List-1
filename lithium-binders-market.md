<p><ul><li>Anode Binder</li><li>Cathode Binder</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The lithium binders market primarily comprises anode and cathode binders, essential components in lithium-ion batteries. Anode binders enhance the mechanical stability and conductivity of the anode materials, typically made from graphite or silicon. Cathode binders, on the other hand, are crucial for ensuring the adhesion of cathode materials, like lithium cobalt oxide or lithium iron phosphate, to the electrode surface. Both binder types significantly impact battery performance, energy density, and longevity, making them vital for the growing electric vehicle and energy storage sectors.</p></p>
