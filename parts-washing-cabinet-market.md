<p><strong>The Parts Washing Cabinet Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Solvent-based Type</li><li>Aqueous-based Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The parts washing cabinet market is categorized into solvent-based and aqueous-based types. Solvent-based cabinets utilize chemical solvents to remove oils, greases, and contaminants from metal parts, offering fast drying times and effective cleaning. However, they may pose environmental and safety concerns due to volatile organic compounds (VOCs). In contrast, aqueous-based cabinets use water-based cleaning solutions, providing a safer alternative with less environmental impact. They are effective for a variety of applications but may require longer drying times and specialized drainage systems.</p></p>
