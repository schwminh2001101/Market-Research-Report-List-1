<p><strong>The Bluetooth Ceiling Speaker Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Active Bluetooth Ceiling Speaker</li><li>Passive Bluetooth Ceiling Speaker</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Bluetooth Ceiling Speaker market comprises two main types: active and passive speakers. Active Bluetooth Ceiling Speakers are standalone units with built-in amplifiers, allowing them to receive audio signals directly from Bluetooth devices without the need for external amplification. In contrast, Passive Bluetooth Ceiling Speakers require an external amplifier to function, as they lack built-in power. These two types cater to different audio needs and installation preferences, with active speakers being simpler to set up, while passive speakers often offer more customization in sound quality.</p></p>
