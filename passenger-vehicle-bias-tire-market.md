<p><p>The Passenger Vehicle Bias Tire market includes two main types: Ordinary Bias Tires and Bias Belted Tires. Ordinary Bias Tires feature crisscrossed ply layers at an angle, offering durability and load-bearing capacity but limited speed performance. Bias Belted Tires add a stabilizing belt layer under the tread, enhancing stability, speed handling, and tread life while maintaining durability. Both types cater to specific vehicle needs, with Ordinary Bias Tires suited for rugged use and Bias Belted Tires balancing performance and comfort for everyday driving.</p></p>
