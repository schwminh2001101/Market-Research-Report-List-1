<p><p>Database as a Service (DBaaS) platforms are categorized primarily into two market types: cloud-based and on-premises. Cloud-based DBaaS offers scalable, managed database solutions hosted on external servers, allowing users to access databases over the internet without investing in hardware or maintenance. This model promotes flexibility and cost-effectiveness. In contrast, on-premises DBaaS involves deploying databases within a company's own infrastructure, providing greater control and customization, but often requires significant upfront investment and ongoing maintenance efforts.</p></p>
