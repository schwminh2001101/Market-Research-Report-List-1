<p><p>The Platinum Thermocouple Wire market features three primary types: Type S, Type R, and Type B. Type S thermocouples, made from platinum-rhodium alloy, are known for their accuracy and stability at high temperatures, making them ideal for laboratory applications. Type R also consists of platinum-rhodium but offers slightly different thermal characteristics. Type B thermocouples, with higher rhodium content, excel in extreme temperature environments. Each type serves specific industrial needs, driven by temperature range, sensitivity, and application requirements.</p></p>
