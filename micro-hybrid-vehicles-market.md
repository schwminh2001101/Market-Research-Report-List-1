<p><p>Micro-hybrid vehicles, also known as mild hybrid vehicles, are a type of hybrid vehicle that utilizes a small electric motor to assist the internal combustion engine (ICE). These vehicles are cost-effective and require minimal changes to traditional ICE designs, making them a popular choice for automakers and consumers seeking greener alternatives without the high costs of full hybrid or electric vehicles.</p><p>The Micro-Hybrid Vehicles Market is experiencing significant growth, driven by increasing environmental regulations, rising fuel prices, and consumer demand for fuel-efficient vehicles.
