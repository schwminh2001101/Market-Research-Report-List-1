Open face helmets cover the top and sides of the head but leave the face exposed, offering a balance of protection and visibility. Half helmets provide minimal coverage, primarily for urban riding. Off-road/motocross helmets are designed with ventilation and lightweight materials for rugged terrains, emphasizing safety and comfort.</p></p>
